About GeriEats

GeriEats is a homebound companionship program between our care partner's at GeriEats and the client who is at risk for frailty and loss of independence. It is a partnership with a comprehensive understanding of various social determinants of health in the older adult population. This program is for the client who chooses to get the health checkup and help they need in the comfort of their own home. Wether if the client needs help with chores, cooking, meal plans delivered to their home or health check ups, GeriEats will be there for you.

We are committed to better understand those dynamics that so often are ignored by the medical community yet most important in the lives of our clients. Through our scientific evidence-based model we search to understand those social determinants of health that matter most for our clients. This careful analysis will allow for our clients to live at home safely and independently.

Each of our care partners are heavily trained in the GeriEats philosophy and fully vested in the care of our organization and local communities. We meet weekly to discuss social measures such as dehydration, constipation, medication adherence, social/physical isolation, missed meals, daily weigh-in's and other desired vitals are taken and entered in our personalized client application. Our geriatrician will extrapolate the various data points and assess for concerns of overall frailty and dehydration, both conditions which are so often missed during basic medical visits.
